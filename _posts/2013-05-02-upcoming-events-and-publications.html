  • Future publications
The Enterico Trio album (with Tim Hodgkinson and Gandolfo Pagano) is ready and will be released on the Die Schachtel label. It is a 6-tracks, ~40 minutes album of non-edited extracts from improvised recording sessions we had in Palermo between the end of 2011 and the beginning of 2012. http://www.dieschachtel.com/
Feedback Systems: an Analytical Framework - a revised and expanded version of the International Computer Music Conference paper (Best Paper Award winner), co-written with Andrea Valle, will be published as an article on Computer Music Journal (CMJ 37:2 - Summer 2013). http://www.mitpressjournals.org/cmj
Turning Perturbation into Emergent Sound, and Sound into Perturbation - an article where I discuss some of my works as well as conceptual and technical aspects of the creative practice implementing complex musical feedback systems. To be published on Interference Journal. http://www.interferencejournal.com/
Osservare la Macchina Performante e Intonare l'Ambiente: Microphone di David Tudor - an analysis of David Tudor’s piece, Microphone,to be published on the italian review Le Arti del Suono. http://leartidelsuono.altervista.org/
