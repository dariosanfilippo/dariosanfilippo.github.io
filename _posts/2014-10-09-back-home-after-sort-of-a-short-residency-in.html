Back home after sort of a short residency in Vienna, kindly supported by Kunst Radio. Me and my friend Peter Kutin have been asked for a composition whose theme was “fire/ice”. After we have realised our materials, me and Peter met in Vienna to work in the studio and compose a long composition which will be broadcast on the 26th of Oct. Considering that the theme is related to phenomena involving chain reactions, we have used feedback as the main mechanism to generate sounds, as well as field recordings of soundscapes related to the theme.
